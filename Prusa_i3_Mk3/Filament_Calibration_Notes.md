# Filamentive rPETG Blue

## Temperature

Tested range of 225-255C in 5C steps to correspond with Filamentive recommendation of 240C+-15C.  
All temperatures acceptable but optimmum seemed to be 240C to 245C. Stuck with 240C as lots of experience with this.

## Max Flow Rate

Tested range of 5-20mm3/s. No major issues encountered. Very slight sheen change observed at height corresponding to 16mm3/s. Settled on 13mm3/s as safe maximum.

## Pressure Advance

**180mm/s (32.3mm3/s) @ 1000mm/s2**  
Best at 0.03

**36mm/s (6.46mm3/s) @ 1000mm/s2**  
Best at 0.025

Chose 0.025 for best all-round

## Flow ratio

Base flow: 0.98
Best quality: -0.01

## Retraction

Tested 0-2mm in 0.1mm.  
No large difference in stringing. Possible benefit around 0.2-0.3mm.
